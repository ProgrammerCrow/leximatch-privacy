LexiMatch – Privacy Policy

Turkmen Digitals

Effective Date: March 17, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to LexiMatch. This Privacy Policy explains how Turkmen Digitals ("we", "us", or "our") handles information in connection with your use of the LexiMatch mobile application (the "App").

By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the App.

2. Information We Collect

LexiMatch does not require account registration and does not collect any personally identifiable information directly.

The following data may be collected automatically through third-party services:

  • Advertising ID – used by Google AdMob to serve relevant advertisements.
  • Device information – such as device type, operating system version, and language settings, collected by AdMob for ad targeting and analytics.
  • App usage data – anonymous interaction data used to improve ad relevance.

All game progress (score, energy, settings) is stored locally on your device only and is never transmitted to our servers.

3. How We Use Information

Information collected through third-party services is used to:

  • Display relevant advertisements within the App.
  • Measure ad performance and improve user experience.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for their own marketing purposes.

4. Advertising (Google AdMob)

LexiMatch uses Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect and use data as described in Google's Privacy Policy. On iOS, you will be asked for permission to track your activity across apps and websites via Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework. You may decline this permission; ads will still be shown but may be less relevant.

For more information on how Google uses data, please visit: https://policies.google.com/privacy

To opt out of interest-based advertising, visit: https://www.aboutads.info/choices

5. Children's Privacy

LexiMatch is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will take steps to delete such information.

6. Data Retention

Since we do not collect personal data on our own servers, there is no retention period. Any data collected by AdMob is subject to Google's data retention policies. Local game data can be deleted at any time by uninstalling the App.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access or request deletion of your personal data held by third-party services.
  • Opt out of personalized advertising via your device settings.
  • Withdraw ATT consent at any time through iOS Settings → Privacy → Tracking.

8. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Google AdMob
  • Expo (runtime framework)

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
